Introduction

The Smoothie Hut

The Smoothie Hut is a small smoothie bar that has an assortment of blended flavors for a refreshing drink or delicious meal. Our jucies are freshly made and there is an endless topping bar for all customers to enjoy.

Target audience

The main target audience are young adults who are interested in fruity smoothies. Young adults who are more into health and fitness may connect with our message more.

The main goal is to supply customers with refreshing drinks that are full of flavors. They can mix and match, crafting their own desires for their pleasure.
